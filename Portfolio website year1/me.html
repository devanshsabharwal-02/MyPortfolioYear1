About Me

I am Devansh Sabharwal and I am currently studying Bsc. Computer science at Queen Mary university of London. I like writing short stories, watching films , playing video games and listening to music. The thing that motivates me the most is to leave some sort of impact through your work which has the power to change and transform somebody’s life. I have always been fascinated by films, videogames and technology as they them have a power to impact people. The most special thing which I love about video games is that through the interactive nature of them which utilize both the technical and narrative components, it creates an exuberant experience born out of pure passion and creativity. They have the power to move people and that’s what I always aspired to do. I am Keen to pursue a career as a cinematic/creative director for video games.
